# Child Compass 5.1 — Voice Review

Fifty conversations for manual review. Cover the names — could one compassionate person have written them all?

## 1

**Parent:** Amy screamed for an hour.

**Child Compass:**

That sounds exhausting.

An hour can feel incredibly long when you're trying to help Amy.

Before we think about what might help, can I ask something.

What was happening just before things escalated?
