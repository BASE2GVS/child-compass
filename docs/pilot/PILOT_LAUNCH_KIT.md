## Pilot onboarding guide (share with families)

1. **Week 1:** Daily check-ins + one debrief. Explore dashboard insights.
2. **Week 2:** Generate a teacher guide or therapist summary. Try Ask Child Compass™ with a real situation.
3. **Feedback:** Use `/pilot-feedback` if enabled, or email support.

## Usage expectations

- Check-ins: 1 per child per day (plan limits apply after trial)
- AI coach: thoughtful questions work best — it uses your real history
- Reports: generate before appointments for maximum value

## Privacy explanation (short)

Your family data is private and isolated. We never sell data. Export or delete anytime from Settings.

## Issue reporting

- In-app: Help → Report a Problem
- Urgent clinical/safety: contact your clinician or local emergency services

## Feedback process

1. Families submit via `/pilot-feedback` or Help forms
2. Review weekly in Admin portal or `data/pilot-feedback.jsonl`
3. Prioritise trust, reliability, and clarity over new features
